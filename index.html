What Happened

Protests Begin

Widespread demonstrations emerged across multiple cities.

Escalation

Reports indicate increasing confrontation between protesters and authorities.

Mass Arrests

Communication Blackout

Reports indicate that internet access was heavily restricted for extended periods, limiting communication between people inside Iran and the outside world.

These disruptions affected the flow of information, making independent verification and real-time reporting significantly more difficult.
